As it became clear that the financial companies are increasingly automating their interaction with the client, and therefore where reorganizing, I was looking for a way out. I found out about Codaisseur and decided to commit myself to become a developer.

Codaisseur: Codaisseur offers a graduate program that enabled me, within a short period of time, to learn what it means to be a webdeveloper. The graduate program is very intensive. Weeks of 70 hours and longer are no exception and the subject matter is diverse and extensive.

Subjects: The traineeship has the following topics: Ruby, RSpec, Rails, JS / Node (incl. ES6), Mocha, Chai, React, Redux, SQL, HTML5, and CSS3 . In addition, the following aspects of development are also discussed: Create reviews and quality assessments, TDD, the basis of product development, UX design, domain modeling and of course teamwork using tools like Atom, git, Github, CI and Slack.
